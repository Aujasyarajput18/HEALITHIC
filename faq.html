Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

You can book a test in three easy ways: (1) Browse our diagnostics page and select the test you need, (2) Choose from our curated health packages, or (3) Contact us directly via phone or email. Once you've selected your test, you can schedule a convenient time for sample collection at home or visit our collection center.
Yes, we offer convenient home sample collection services. Our trained phlebotomists will visit your preferred location at a time that suits you. Simply book your test and select the home collection option. We ensure all safety protocols and hygiene standards are strictly followed during the collection process.
Most routine tests are processed and reports are available within 24-48 hours. Some specialized tests may take up to 72 hours. Critical tests can be expedited and are available within 6-12 hours. You'll receive an email notification as soon as your reports are ready, and you can access them through our secure online portal.
Yes, all our partner laboratories are NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories) certified. We also maintain ISO 15189 standards and other international quality certifications. This ensures the highest accuracy and reliability of all test results.
Fasting requirements depend on the specific test you're taking. Tests like lipid profile, blood sugar, and comprehensive health packages typically require 12 hours of fasting (water is allowed). However, many tests like CBC, thyroid function, and vitamin tests don't require fasting. Detailed preparation instructions are provided when you book a test.
Once your test results are ready, you'll receive an email notification with a secure link to access your reports. You can log in to our online portal using your registered email and password. Reports are available in PDF format and can be downloaded, printed, or shared with your healthcare provider. You can also access your test history anytime through your account.
Yes, we offer free consultation with our experienced healthcare professionals to help you understand your test results. You can schedule a consultation after receiving your reports. Our doctors will explain what your results mean, answer any questions, and provide guidance on next steps if needed.
We accept multiple payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery (for home collection). You can pay online when booking your test or pay in cash when our phlebotomist visits for sample collection. All online transactions are secure and encrypted.
Yes, our health packages offer significant savings compared to booking individual tests separately. Packages are designed to provide comprehensive health screening at discounted rates, typically saving you 20-35% compared to individual test prices. Additionally, packages ensure all related tests are done together, providing a complete health picture.
Absolutely. We take patient privacy and data security very seriously. All your health information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with HIPAA and other data protection regulations. Your test results are accessible only to you and healthcare providers you authorize. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Simply contact us via phone or email, or use the online portal to manage your appointments. Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee. Refunds for prepaid tests will be processed according to our refund policy.
If your test results show any abnormal values, don't panic. Many factors can influence test results. We recommend scheduling a consultation with our healthcare professionals or your primary care physician. They can help interpret the results in context, consider your medical history, and recommend appropriate follow-up tests or treatments if needed.
