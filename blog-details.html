10 Essential Vitamins for Optimal Health

March 15, 2024

Summary: Discover the key vitamins your body needs to function at its best. This guide covers essential vitamins and their benefits.


1. Vitamin A

Crucial for vision, immune function, and cell growth. Found in carrots, sweet potatoes, and spinach.

2. Vitamin B Complex

Includes B1, B2, B12, and others. Vital for energy and brain function.

3. Vitamin C

A powerful antioxidant that supports immune function. Found in citrus fruits.

4. Vitamin D

Essential for bone health. Best source is sunlight.
